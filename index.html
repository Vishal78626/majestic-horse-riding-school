About us

Majestic Horse Riding School

Majestic Horse Riding School aims to instruct students in safe and respectful horse riding throughout their entire educational journey. We provide horse riding lessons on our school horses, or you may bring your own horse and utilize our facilities. Our passion lies in establishing the finest Horse Riding School, and Majestic Riding School equips students with the tools and support required to help them reach their full potential.

We Offer

Majestic Horse Riding School offers a variety of exceptional services, including Rider Safety, private lessons, and Beginner Lessons, among others. We have absolute confidence in the potential of each of our students and provide these services to enhance their education and develop new skills. Our lessons take place in a spacious sand arena enclosed by a protective barrier. For further information, please don't hesitate to contact us.
